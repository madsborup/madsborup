Junier is a tool I designed, built, and launched as an independent founder - from initial concept to final product. It helps teams build better products with real-time, feedback-powered insights in their workflow.

I started Junier because I saw a fundamental change in how teams do product work due to AI. As AI takes over more of the execution - generating designs, writing code - what will matter even more is understanding users, craft, and vision. And I wanted to build for that. 

See more at [https://junier.co](https://junier.co).

### Building Product Knowledge

A core technical challenge of Junier was generating unique, product-specific data powering the contextual insights generation. With sessions, users run live video meetings with participants and their prototypes or live website. Junier then creates data about the product and the participants by connecting what happens on screen to what participants say and do. This data then drives real-time insights where the designer is working, using image and text data to infer work context and matching it with feedback data.

This involved building an AI pipeline handling recording and storing session data (audio, video), multi-modal AI classification, embeddings, a vector database, and LLMs.
